# Governance

Harness Evals is currently a maintainer-led project. Dhiman Seal is the initial maintainer and release authority.

The maintainer owns roadmap decisions, contributor access, release publication, security response, and final acceptance of changes. Decisions prioritize evaluator correctness, security, reproducibility, portability, simplicity, and maintenance cost in that order when tradeoffs cannot be eliminated.

Material decisions are made in public issues or pull requests when security or private holdout confidentiality does not require a private channel. Public schema changes, statistical rules, provider authority, and release policy require a written rationale, compatibility decision, executable evidence, and changelog entry.

Contributors earn broader review or maintenance responsibility through sustained technically sound work, respectful review, and demonstrated care for the evaluator's trust boundaries. Additional maintainers may be named in this file through a reviewed pull request.

No maintainer may approve a release-authoritative private holdout alone. Holdout preparation requires the independent reviewer identities and evidence defined by the sealed-plan protocol.
